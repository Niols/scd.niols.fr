32-bar jig

Get the score of the tune as PDF.

Composed by Nicolas “Niols” Jeannerod in August 2019.

I wrote this tune during Summer School 2019. It was an assignment given by Ian Muir as part of the course for class musicians. The week was very busy and the only time I found to compose the tune while I was on my way to the centre of St. Andrews to go print music sets.

Recommended for: On The Way To The Printing Shop, by Giulio Dujany, Pascaline Latour and Nicolas “Niols” Jeannerod.
